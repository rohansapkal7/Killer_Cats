Killer Cats is a 2 Player, 3 Player and 4 Player strategic turn-based elimination game where, you have to survive at last by eliminating other (opponent) Cats. You have to apply your own stratergies along with Creative Powerups and Movement direction to dominate the field

Every player have their own console to jump their cat one block in top, down, left, right, top-left, top-right, bottom-left and bottom-right direction(s). Also it has the power button to use powers. You can kill a cat by simply jumping on it or using different powers.

Mocchu-Slime can be thrown in range of 6 tiles from player in all directions. Every target within the range will get sticked to ground and will be unable to move for next three (3) turns.

Iron Mess is a heavy spiked ball, that can be thrown in top, bottom, left, right direction(s) and its range is upto designated direction boundry. This killer ball is able to one-shot-kill your rivals.

Bubu's shield will protect you from the power attacks like Mocchu-Slime, Iron Mess and Power Gem. Its effect is only for one-time from collecting it. Unfortunately it can't avoid Tullu's Book and Jump attacks.

Tullu's Book can teleport you in designated direction boundry. So you can use it for both Escaping as well as Attack purpose.

The Power Gem is able to create a Dark-Hole on Caster's next directed block. This Dark-Hole kills every player stepped in it and it doesn't have a fade-out time. So, use it wisely and don't be get hunted by your own.
